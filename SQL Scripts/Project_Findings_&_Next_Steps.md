# Retail Sales Analysis - Findings & Recommendations

## Objective

Analyze retail sales data to identify revenue drivers, profitability patterns, regional performance, and discount effectiveness.

---

# Key Findings

## Revenue Analysis

### Regional Performance

* East Region generated approximately 39% of total revenue.
* South Region generated the lowest revenue among all regions.

### Product Performance

Top revenue-generating products:

* Books
* Television
* Footwear
* Set Dairy
* Traditional Laptop

---

## Profitability Analysis

Highest profit-generating products:

* Footwear
* Set Dairy

These products contribute significantly to overall profitability and should be prioritized for inventory planning.

---

## Discount Analysis

### High Discount + Low Revenue Products

Products such as:

* Vegetables
* US Snacks

received relatively high discounts but generated low revenue.

Finding:

Current discount strategies for these products appear ineffective.

---

### High Discount + High Revenue Products

Electronics products maintained strong revenue performance despite discounts.

Finding:

Discount strategies appear more effective in electronics categories.

---

## Regional Insights

### East Region

* Highest revenue contributor.
* Strong customer demand.
* Opportunity to strengthen customer retention and loyalty programs.

### South Region

* Lowest revenue contributor.
* Requires additional investigation into customer acquisition and product demand.

---

# Business Recommendations

## Inventory Optimization

Increase inventory allocation for:

* Footwear
* Set Dairy

These products generate strong profits and demand.

---

## Discount Strategy Review

Reduce aggressive discounting on:

* Vegetables
* US Snacks

Current discounts do not appear to generate proportional revenue growth.

---

## Electronics Promotions

Continue targeted promotional campaigns for electronics products where discounts appear effective.

---

## Customer Retention

Launch loyalty programs and retention campaigns in East Region to retain high-value customers.

---

## Regional Growth

Develop targeted marketing campaigns in South Region to improve customer engagement and sales performance.
